First off, speed is of the essence. Nobody has time for a browser that lags like a snail in molasses. You want something that zips through tasks quicker than a caffeine-fueled squirrel. Consider benchmarks and real-world performance tests. Some browsers are optimized to handle heavy loads, while others might buckle under the weight of multiple tabs.

Secondly, look at compatibility. Does the browser support all the nifty productivity tools and extensions you rely on?
